Importance of Mental Health

Mental Heal is an integral and essential component of health. It is important at every stage of life, from childhood to adolescence through adulthood.

It is a state of well-being in which and individual realises his or her own abilities, can cope with the narmal stresses of life, work productively, and is able to make a comtribution to his or her community.
